Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment through our online booking system, by calling our office at (555) 123-4567, or by sending an email to schedule@studentcenter.edu. For urgent matters, we also offer walk-in hours every weekday from 10 AM to 2 PM.

Are your services confidential?

Yes, all counseling sessions and services provided by the Student Support Center are strictly confidential. We adhere to professional ethical standards and privacy laws to ensure your information remains private and secure.

Do I need to pay for counseling services?

Most of our services are provided free of charge to enrolled students. Some specialized assessments or workshops may have a nominal fee, but we work to keep these affordable. We can also connect you with insurance-covered services when appropriate.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

For your first appointment, please bring your student ID, any relevant academic records or documentation, and a list of questions or concerns you'd like to address. If you're seeking career counseling, bringing a draft resume or list of interests can be helpful.
